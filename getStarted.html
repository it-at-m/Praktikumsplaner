Get Started ​

Welcome to the official documentation of the Praktikumsplaner! Whether you're a developer looking to contribute or a user aiming to integrate our project into your workflow, this guide will help you get started.

Overview ​

Praktikumsplaner is designed to organize junior staff and their internships during their vocational training.

To get the most out of the Praktikumsplaner, we've organized this documentation into several key sections:

  • About: Learn more about the project's background and goals behind it.
  • Features: Explore the full list of features and capabilities of Praktikumsplaner.
  • Technical Documentation: Dive deep into technical details, including setup, configuration, and architectural decisions.
  • GitHub Repository: Access the source code, contribute, and track issues.

Getting Started ​

  1. Installation: Follow our technical setup to set up the Praktikumsplaner on your system.

  2. Contribute: Interested in contributing? Check out our contributor's guide for details on how you can make a difference.

  3. Support: Need help? Open an issue to ask for assistance.
